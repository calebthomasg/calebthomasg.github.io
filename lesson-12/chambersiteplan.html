Site Purpose: This is is being built for the purpose of business owners of the Preston Idaho community to come together and support local business. The site will provide a way for business owners to join the Chamber of Commerce. It will provide a way for local businesses to get advertised. It will advertise local business events. It will list all the businesses in Preston. It will have a forecast for the town. It will have contact information for the Chamber.
