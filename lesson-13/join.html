MEMBERSHIP LEVEL

Every member must choose a level of membership in the Chamber of Commerce. Each level up in membership provides more benefits to you and your business.

NON-PROFIT

  • Enhanced web listings
  • Community event calendar that posts to multiple social media platforms
  • Grand openings, groundbreakings and ribbon cutting ceremonies
  • Membership mailing address list
  • Email marketing
  • Authorized use of Chamber logo
  • Attend Chamber events
  • Booth access at expos
  • Business education seminars
  • Local business discounts

BRONZE

  • Non-profit Level Benefits PLUS
  • Ability to host a networking event
  • Coupon in the Preston Coupon Book
  • Tiered placement in the Preston Chamber of Commerce directory
  • Banner ad in the Preston Citizen

SILVER

  • Bronze Level Benefits PLUS
  • Facebook Live spotlight
  • Digital ad display at visitors center
  • $200 advertising credit
  • 25% sponsorship discount on all events

GOLD

  • Silver Level Benefits PLUS
  • Chamber concierge services
  • Logo on banner at all Chamber events
  • Four complimentary headshots
  • $400 advertising credit
  • 50% sponsorship discount on all events
